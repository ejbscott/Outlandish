A podcast for trail runners and other outlandish folk

Outlandish is a new podcast by Ed Scott, focusing on the burgeoning trail running scene in London and Southeast England.

Each episode, Ed talks to a mover (and sometimes a shaker) in this thriving scene, in the hopes of establishing that adventure needn't be just the pastime of mountain folk, but is available to anyone within strolling distance of a line of dirt.

Tune in below, or wherever you get your podcasts.
